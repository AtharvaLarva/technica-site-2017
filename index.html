Ladies, Welcome to Tech.

All-women hackathon. November 7-8, 2015. College Park, MD.

This is tech culture beyond hello world.

Technica is the University of Maryland’s first all-ladies hackathon. Over the duration of 24 hours, ladies are immersed in tech culture and encouraged to exercise their imagination to create interesting and innovative hacks. Technica is diverging from the typical hackathon as we encourage gender diversification, and ask sponsors to hack alongside students in order to build stronger connections between students, sponsors, and the tech world.
